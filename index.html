Rules

Welcome to Hangman! First choose a category to get started. You will then be redirected to a screen dependent on the category you chose. You will then have 6 lives to guess the word letter by letter. Every letter you guess that is not in the word will cost you a life. Don't worry if you get stuck, you can ask for a hint by clicking on the Hint button Click to regenerate a new word if you want to stay in the same category or click Play Again to be brought back to the home screen. Good luck and have fun :)
